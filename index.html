Help V²Configuration

V²Configuration Pane

The left pane of the V²Configurator assists you in making choices which in turn will create your Custom PC's Configuration.

First choose your Field of Application

Navigate to Field of Application. 1) Unfold Wizard, 2) Unfold "Field of Application

For more Information read Advice

Within the "Field of Application" unfold Advice and it will guide you on how to proceed. In fact every section that you unfold contains Advice, ready at your fingertip in case you need it.

If there is a Triangle you can unfold it

Its kind of like Easter Eggs. We have hidden a lot of goodies. Click the triangle to unfold.

Navigate by unfolding and collapsing

By unfolding you dig deeper. You can unfold as many Chapters, Sections and Sub Sections as you like. When collapsing the parent the Configurator will remember the state of folding for its children. In case you get lost, collapse and scroll up to reach the top level, from where you can dig down again.

Color Code

The 1. Chapter (Orange) is the Wizard. With a few clicks you determine the broad strokes of your configuration. Chapter 2 ... 5 are the Parts provided by us (Blue). Chapter 6 ... 7 (Cyan) contain the parts provided by Third Parties, such as Amazon / Newegg or your favorite shop for PC parts.

Help V²Configuration

V²Summary Pane

This Pane shows all your parts which your Custom PC will be made off. On a wide screen it is shown to the right. On a small screen to the bottom of the page.

Sub-Summaries

For you convenience the costs are grouped. Each group can be unfolded or collapsed to assist your overview.

Total Cost

Total cost is shown on the bottom. Please read the disclaimer when unfolded to avoid any confusion.

Check Out

Pressing the Checkout Button opens the Checkout Window, which list all items. When clicking the item a new tab opens with its online shop page. Within the "Checkout Village Parts" window all links will open our parts on Indiegogo. Within the "Checkout Third Party" Window all links will open the respective 3rd Party Parts on Amazon or Newegg. We have no association with either shop. You are welcome to buy the same or similar part in another shop of your preference.
